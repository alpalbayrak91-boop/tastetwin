# Chrome Web Store Publish Checklist

1. Open the Chrome Web Store Developer Dashboard.
2. Choose **Add new item** and upload `public/tastetwin-extension.zip`.
3. Store listing: paste `store/listing-tr.md`, choose Social & Communication, and upload the prepared icon, screenshot, and 440x280 promo tile.
4. Privacy practices: copy the single-purpose, permission-justification, and disclosure answers from `store/privacy-practices.md`.
5. Distribution: choose Public, all regions, and free.
6. Test instructions: keep TasteTwin open, open a Letterboxd profile owned by the tester, click scan 1, and verify the local app receives the counts.
7. Submit for review with deferred publishing disabled if immediate release after approval is desired.
